How Error Level Analysis Works:
  1. Load Original: Your image is loaded and converted to RGB
  2. JPEG Recompression: Image is saved at exactly 90% quality
  3. Calculate Difference: Pixel-by-pixel comparison is performed
  1. Amplify Brightness: Differences are enhanced for visibility
  2. Generate Heatmap: Bright areas indicate potential tampering
Tip: Brighter/hotter regions in the result indicate higher compression inconsistency and potential tampering.
